# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)** in full.

## Summary

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone. We expect community members to demonstrate empathy, respect differing viewpoints, give and accept constructive feedback gracefully, take responsibility for mistakes, and focus on what is best for the overall community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, personal attacks, publishing private information without permission, sexualized language or imagery, and conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported confidentially to the org leadership at **mike@leys.dev**.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Community leadership is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

## Enforcement

Community leaders will follow the four-tier enforcement guidelines defined in Contributor Covenant 2.1: Correction, Warning, Temporary Ban, Permanent Ban.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces — every repo in the organization, GitHub Discussions, issue and PR threads, and any official community events — and also when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.
