# Experience Extension Community

> **A community-driven open-source project. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected to Ellucian Company L.P. "Ellucian", "Ellucian Experience", "Banner", "Colleague", and related marks are trademarks of Ellucian Company L.P. and are used here for descriptive purposes only.**

Open-source extensions for **Ellucian Experience®**, built and shared by higher-ed institutions. We're a community of developers from colleges and universities who use Ellucian Experience and want to stop solving the same problems in isolation.

If your institution has built a degree-progress card, an advisor dashboard, a financial-aid widget — anything you'd be willing to share — there's a home for it here.

---

## Browse extensions

See [**CATALOG.md**](https://github.com/experience-extension-community/.github/blob/main/CATALOG.md) in the hub repo for a curated index of every extension in the org, grouped by category.

You can also search the org by tag — every repo here uses topic tags like `ellucian-experience`, `experience-card`, `experience-page`, plus subject tags (`advising`, `financial-aid`, `student-services`, etc.).

## Build a new extension

Click **Use this template** on **[`experience-extension-template`](https://github.com/experience-extension-community/experience-extension-template)** and you'll have a fully scaffolded Ellucian Experience extension repo — with linting, CI, accessibility checks, branding tokens, sample card, sample page, and an institutional setup guide — in about thirty seconds.

Then walk through the `REPLACE_THESE.md` checklist in your new repo to make it yours.

## About this community

This open-source community was founded in 2025 by Michael Leys at **[Florida Polytechnic University](https://floridapoly.edu)** to break institutions out of the pattern of solving the same Ellucian Experience extension problems in isolation.

The community is independent and welcomes peer institutions as co-maintainers, sponsors, and contributors. Florida Poly's role is as initiating institution — not owner. Decisions are made per [GOVERNANCE.md](https://github.com/experience-extension-community/.github/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md), which transitions to a Steering Committee with one seat per actively contributing institution as the community grows.
